Get Involved

We are always looking for new volunteers and we offer internship opportunities, so please contact us if you would like to get involved.

If you are interested in incorporating this programme into your school or would like your staff trained, please get in touch with us at pydfoundationsa@gmail.com.

If you would like to discuss a partnership between the PYDF and your university or organisation, please email us at pydfoundationsa@gmail.com.
